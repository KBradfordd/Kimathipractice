My Passion For Reducing Lead Pollution

I am passionate about reducing lead pollution because of the neighborhood I grew up in. For a while, I always just assumed my neighborhood was filled with pollutants because of how poor it was and because there was a lot of pollution in the poor areas around the city like mine. As a rising junior, I attended a program where I went around and collected soil to sample it. I sent these samples back to a lab where they were tested for lead. When these results came in, they ended up being littered with red samples, showing that my neighborhood was highly polluted with lead. Though eradicating lead pollution is one of my passions, I feel a passion for environmental science as a whole.

Lead Pollution and the Dangers of it

Lead pollution is caused by poor building practices and high amounts of pollution. Two issues that tend to run rampant in impoverished communities around the United States. The fact that these practices disproportionately affect poor communities forces the communities to live near these high levels of pollution.
